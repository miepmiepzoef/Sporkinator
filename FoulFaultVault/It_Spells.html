🔹 Consonants (primitives)

A=action/initiate, B=barrier/bound, C=create, D=direction, E=exit/exterior, F=flow/force, G=gather/ground, H=hold/hide, I=inward/self, J=join, K=kinetic/cut, L=layer/level, M=motion, N=negation/not, O=orbit/cycle, P=push, Q=query, R=repeat/rotate, S=separate/split, T=terminus/tool, U=under/container, V=void, W=wave/water (or UU if toggled), X=cross/negation, Y=branch/why, Z=zero/null

🔸 Vowel operators

A = left initiates right
E = left exits to right
I = swap left & right
O = repeat left once, then right
U = unify left & right
Initial vowel → treat as consonant; final vowel → attach 'exit' flag

📌 Special rules

• Emotions (e.g., ANGER, SAD, FEAR, LOVE, JOY) → implicit I prefix
• "am" before emotion → injects M (motion)
• Pronouns: I→I, YOU→U, HE/SHE/IT→H
• Function words (a, an, the, is, are, be, of, to, for, and, or, but) → inert (quoted)
