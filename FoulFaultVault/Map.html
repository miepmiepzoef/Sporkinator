Synthesis of Monroe, theosophical and comtemporary esoteric narratives

NEUTRAL MAP: NON-PHYSICAL TERRAIN

This map compiles descriptions from Robert Monroe’s explorations, later Monroe Institute explorer materials, theosophical sources, and modern channeled accounts. It is offered as a reference, not a belief system.

FOCUS LEVELS (addresses in consciousness)

FOCUS 10: Mind awake, body asleep. Deep physical relaxation with alert awareness.
The entry state for non-physical exploration. (Monroe)
FOCUS 12: Expanded awareness beyond the body.
Gateway for receiving information, problem-solving, and remote viewing. (Monroe)
FOCUS 15: No time / no space.
Access to universal knowledge; psi phenomena become effortless. (Monroe)
FOCUS 21: The edge of time/space.
Bridge to other dimensions; access to information outside linear time. (Monroe)
FOCUS 22: Chaotic, unstructured zone.
Described as where consciousness goes under influence of drugs, alcohol, anesthesia, or severe disorientation (coma, dementia). Not a structured plane. (Monroe)
FOCUS 23: The Stuck Zone.
Souls recently deceased but unaware of death, obsessed with earthly attachments, can remain trapped. (Monroe)
FOCUS 24-26: Belief System Territories.
Self-created realities formed by shared religious or ideological beliefs. Inhabitants group by like conviction. Function as consensus-based environments. (Monroe Institute explorer synthesis)
FOCUS 27: The Park.
Described as an artificial reception center created by human minds to ease the trauma of death. A transitional waiting area. (Monroe)
FOCUS 34/35: The Council / The Gathering.
Location associated with non-physical intelligences said to observe Earth. Nature and intent ambiguous. (Post-Monroe channeled material)

THE SEVEN PLANES (Theosophical model, reinterpreted by later esoteric sources)

PHYSICAL PLANE: Dense matter. Where embodied life occurs.
ASTRAL PLANE: Interpenetrates the physical. Divided into seven sub-layers. Heavily influenced by thought-forms and described as partially artificial or manipulated.
MENTAL PLANE: Higher thought forms. Used for programming and implant insertion according to some accounts.
BUDDHIC PLANE: Intuitional. Access said to require clearing certain psychological patterns (e.g., victim-victimizer dynamic).
ATMIC PLANE: Spiritual will. Often described as blocked by artificial grids.
ANUPADAKA PLANE: Monadic. Pure being.
ADI PLANE: Divine. The "outside" of the matrix.
ENGINEERED INFRASTRUCTURE (as described in modern esoteric narratives, e.g., Ascension Glossary, Guardian Alliance material)
REVERSAL 55 GRID: Artificial construct based on Fibonacci mathematics. Spins at 55/53 (compared to natural merkaba 33⅓ / 11⅔). Said to be unstable and on a finite timeline.
FALSE ASCENSION MATRIX: Inorganic technology installed into astral plane. Purported to force reincarnation via a "false white light" tunnel. All afterlife reception centers (including Focus 27) are understood by some accounts to be subsystems within this matrix, not exits from it.
VICTIM-VICTIMIZER SOFTWARE: Psychological operating system; cycles between powerlessness and domination. Generates emotional energy (fear, hatred) harvestable by certain entities.
ALIEN IMPLANTS: Crystalline or technological devices placed in energy body, often during childhood. Reported to anchor mind control and distort perception.
BELIEF SYSTEM TRAPS: Consensus hallucinations that become self-sustaining. Belief in a specific heaven causes one to go there after death and remain until reincarnated.

ACCESS METHODS: SOBER VS. SUBSTANCE

SOBER (e.g., Monroe audio / Hemi-Sync): Controlled, intentional navigation to known addresses. User retains ability to refuse "false light" or deceptive entities. Requires training and practice.
SUBSTANCE-INDUCED (e.g., DMT, LSD, ketamine): Often described as uncontrolled entry into Focus 22. No map or protection; encounters may be chaotic or deceptive. Consent is considered uninformed because the user does not know the nature of the destination. (Note: Mapping drug states specifically to Focus 22 is a later interpolation, not directly from Monroe.)
RITUAL SUBSTANCE USE (e.g., ayahuasca with shamanic framework): Intermediate case. Ritual provides some structure, but portals may still be opened unintentionally.

ORIGINAL (NON-ARTIFICIAL) BLUEPRINTS

DRAGON GRID (Krystal Spiral): Organic geometry (45-degree tri-tone wave). Runs on Base-12 mathematics. Sustains itself from Zero Point (Source energy). Described as the original template before hijacking.
ZERO POINT FIELD: The aspect of consciousness that was never inside any artificial matrix. Accessible by revoking all contracts and agreements.
LOCALE III (Monroe): Beyond Focus levels. Described by Monroe as another reality system outside standard focus training. Later sources have associated it with beings such as "Orion dissenters" or "Dragon people" – this is a synthesis, not original to Monroe.

NOTES ON NAVIGATION

Explicit, informed consent revocation is required to exit the reincarnation loop according to some accounts. Fibonacci-based layers are described as designed to collapse; the collapse is reportedly in progress. Belief systems create real planes – but those planes are described by some as artificial consensus traps. Before entering any "light", one may ask: "Does this light come from my own true source, or from any external system?"

This map is descriptive, not prescriptive. Verification is left to the individual through sober practice.
